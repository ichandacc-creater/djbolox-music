About DJ Bolox

DJ Bolox has been producing music since 2011, building a reputation as one of Zambia’s most consistent and innovative producers. Over the years, he has collaborated with some of the nation’s top artists, including Vinchenzo, Xaven, Chef 187, and Umusepela Crown, delivering beats that have defined and elevated the sound of modern Zambian music.

With more than a decade of experience, his mission goes beyond production — he strives to craft music that inspires, uplifts, and empowers artists to reach new heights. Today, DJ Bolox continues to push boundaries, blending creativity with technical mastery to create productions that resonate not only in Zambia but across the world.
